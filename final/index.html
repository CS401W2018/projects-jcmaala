LeBron James

LeBron James is an American basketball player who became famous from playing in various NBA teams in the NBA. The NBA is one of the biggest, if not the biggest basketball league in the world. Drafted in 2003, both fans and media have been eyeing to James as he showed great skills and potential in becoming an NBA superstar. His first team was in Cleveland Cavaliers were he was able to lead the team into the finals after being in the team for four seasons. They were playing against the San Antonio Spurs were they lost.

After some season with Cleveland Cavaliers, he made a hard decision to request for trade, which then led him to his next team which is the Miami Heat. It was a phenomenal trade since Chris Bosh, a superstar, was also traded from the Raptors. With the current superstar in the Heat, Dwane Wade, and the addition of the two, they were able to be in multiple finals appearance, of which they were able to win twice.

LeBron James did not forgot his promise to Cleveland since he got drafted by them. In July 11, 2014, he again made a decision that shocked many NBA fans, as he decided to play again for the Cleveland Cavaliers. At the time, Cavaliers doesn't have key players that could help LeBron bring the team to the finals aside from the rising star Kyrie Irving. As the season went on, the team was able to acquire key players like Kevin Love, JR Smith, Iman Shumpert, and the others that helped him lift the Cavs into their first final appearance for more than ten years.

In the finals, they face the Golden State Warriors, that soon became their Finals rival for the next six years. Both of these teams face each other for six straight seasons, having the Cavaliers only won one of them.

Sooner LeBron decided to move to a different team, this time playing with the Los Angeles Lakers. Lakers was able to acquire Anthony Davis, a phenomenal bigman, and other veteran NBA stars like Rajon Rondo and Dwight Howard that helped the team won their first championship in more than ten years. It was the fourth championship title of LeBron James which is very significant since they played the season during the pandemic.
