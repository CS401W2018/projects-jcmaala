Structural Tags

Question: What goes in the Nsav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go to the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

Answer: Navigational links are the ones that goes into the Nav element in the HTML. The Nav can definitely be placed in the header, but it doesn't necessarily mean it has to do so all the time. Nav usually doesn't contain links to another website, but to websites that are connected to the index or root site.

Question: What goes in Main?

Answer: The Main enclose the primary content of a webpage. Inside the Main element is where you can see most of the content in a webpage whenever it is published online.

Question: What goes in the Footer?

Answer: The footer element should contain information such as contact numbers and address, the copyright information of the page, social media links, and also site credits.
