Engagement Questions:

1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags are the elements in HTML that you can move around, while inline tags are strictly just inline with your HTML and cannot be moved around.

2. What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is the space outside the border of an element, border is the line that surrounds the content of the element, and padding is the space inside the border.

3. What are the units of measurement you can use in CSS?

The CSS uses the following units: px for pixels, cm for centimeters, in for inches, and pt for points.

4. When would you use a figure tag?

We used the figure tab whenever we want to group a photo so it can be movable in the HTML.
